Active ingredients

Leaf extracts of;

• Citronella (Limonene, Geraniol, Citral)

• Lantana (Lantadenes, Flavonoids, Triterpenoids)

• Lemongrass (Citral, Myrcene, Geraniol, Flavonoids)

   

What is LEMILANTA HERBAL OINTMENT and what is it used for?

For External Use Only

This ointment is specifically formulated and tested to provide mosquito repellency. The active compounds in citronella and lemongrass oils have been clinically shown to disrupt mosquito olfactory receptors, helping to protect against mosquito bites..

      

Note: Other potential medicinal properties of the ingredients such as wound healing, antimicrobial, anti-inflammatory, or antioxidant effects, have not been assessed or substantiated for this product.

   

Before you use LEMILANTA HERBAL OINTMENT

Do not use:

• If you are allergic to citronella, lantana, lemongrass, or similar plant oils.

• On broken, damaged, or deep wounds.

• Near eyes, mouth, or other mucous membranes

Take special care:

• If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, consult your healthcare provider before use.

• If you develop skin redness, irritation, or rash, stop using the ointment and seek medical advice.

• Avoid prolonged sun exposure on areas treated with this ointment due to sensitizing properties of lantana extract

   

How to use LEMILANTA HERBAL OINTMENT

• Apply a thin layer of ointment on exposed skin where mosquito bites are a concern.

• Start by testing a small amount on the inner forearm and wait 24 hours to check for any reaction.

• Use as needed(Locations where bites want to be avoided).

• After application, wash your hands thoroughly to avoid accidental contact with eyes or ingestion.

  

Possible side effects

Like all medicines, Lemilanta Herbal Ointment can cause side effects, although not everybody gets them. These may include:

• Mild skin irritation, redness, or itching.

• Allergic reaction in sensitive individuals (rare).

• Increased sensitivity to sunlight on treated skin (due to lantana).

If you notice any serious reactions, such as swelling or difficulty breathing, seek medical help immediately.

   

Other important information

• Store the ointment in a cool, dark place away from heat and sunlight to keep it effective.

• This ointment may enhance effects of other topical antimicrobials or anti-inflammatory agents.

• Use with caution if taking sedatives, blood thinners, diabetes medications, or drugs metabolized by liver enzymes CYP3A4 or CYP1A1.

• Avoid excessive sun exposure after use due to photosensitizing potential of lantana.

Keep out of reach of children.

  

Summary for safe use

• Use only on intact skin.

• Avoid eyes and mucous membranes.

• Use as mosquito repellent only.

• Discontinue if irritation occurs.

• Consult a doctor if pregnant or breastfeeding

If you have any questions or need more information, ask your doctor or pharmacist.
